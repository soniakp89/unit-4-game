Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total scrore.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number, lose the game if total score goes above the random number.

The value for each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
